Principal:
The principal is the original amount borrowed for a loan or the original amount invested.In calculating for principal,  divide the interest over the multiplication
of rate and  time

Rate:
Rate is the rate of interest at which the principal amount is given to someone for a certain time, the rate of interest can be 5%, 10%, or 13%, etc.In calculating
for rate,divide the interest over the multiplication principal and time


Time:
This is the period from the beginning when the money was borrowed to the period that when the money should be returned with the additional amount (interest).In
calclating the time, divide interest over the multiplication of principal and rate


Simple Interest:
Simple interest is an interest charge that borrowers pay lenders for a loan. It is calculated using the principal only and does not include compounding interest.
In calculating simple interest,multiply the principal by rate and time ,divide result by 100
